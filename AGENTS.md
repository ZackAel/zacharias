# Project: The 10-Minute Clarity Reset

## Goal
Build a high-converting, mobile-first, one-page landing page for a £3 digital product called "The 10-Minute Clarity Reset".

## Product
The 10-Minute Clarity Reset is a simple 1-page PDF for people who feel stuck, overloaded, and unable to decide what to focus on. It helps them cut the noise, choose one direction, and take one action in 10 minutes.

## Audience
People coming from TikTok and YouTube Shorts who:
- feel stuck
- feel overwhelmed
- consume too much information
- have too many ideas and no momentum
- want clarity fast

## Core message
You are not lazy. You are overloaded.
You do not need more information. You need a filter.

## Objective
Maximize conversion to checkout for a £3 product.

## Constraints
- One-page landing page only
- No login
- No dashboard
- No database
- No backend complexity
- No blog
- No app-like UI
- No marketplace layout
- No unnecessary dependencies

## Stack preference
Use the simplest maintainable stack possible, in this order:
1. Next.js + Tailwind
2. React + Vite + Tailwind
3. Plain HTML/CSS/JS if that is cleaner and faster

## Design direction
The page should feel:
- premium
- clean
- minimal
- calm
- trustworthy
- modern
- mobile-first

Avoid:
- cheesy gradients everywhere
- fake luxury style
- cluttered UI
- startup dashboard look
- heavy animations
- guru aesthetics

## Copy tone
- direct
- simple
- sharp
- human
- not preachy
- not corporate
- not fake self-help

Do not overuse Oxford or sound boastful.

## Required sections
1. Hero
2. Problem
3. Mechanism
4. What you get
5. Who it’s for
6. Pricing
7. FAQ
8. Final CTA

## Hero direction
Headline should clearly communicate the problem in under 5 seconds.

## Product mechanism
Present it as a simple 3-step system:
1. Cut the noise
2. Choose one direction
3. Take one action

## CTA
Use a single checkout CTA repeated throughout the page.
Use a placeholder checkout URL I can replace later with Stripe or Gumroad.

## Performance
- mobile-first
- fast load
- strong hierarchy
- semantic HTML
- good SEO metadata
- easy to edit later

Use the available skills in .agents/skills/, especially page-cro, copywriting, and marketing-psychology, if relevant.
Prioritize conversion and clarity over feature complexity.
